Frequently asked questions

What's included in a Quizlet Plus plan?

With Quizlet Plus, you'll get:

- 3 practice tests per month
- 3 Q&A and textbook solutions per month (\*limits apply)
- 20 rounds of Learn questions per month
- Unlimited access to all games and activities
- Millions of flashcards covering a wide range of subjects

* * *

What's included in a Quizlet Plus Unlimited plan?

With Quizlet Plus Unlimited, you'll get:

- Complete access to practice tests
- Millions of textbook solutions (\*limits apply)
- Unlimited rounds of Learn questions
- Unlimited access to all games and activities
- Millions of flashcards across all subjects
- Ad-free studying for a distraction-free experience

* * *

Can I try Quizlet Plus and Quizlet Plus Unlimited?

You can only use your free trial on one paid tier (Plus or Plus Unlimited).

* * *

What is a Family plan?

A Quizlet Family plan allows the primary account holder (the plan manager) to share their Quizlet Plus Unlimited annual subscription with up to 4 additional plan members.Each plan member has their own Quizlet account (and can connect an existing one to this subscription) with complete access to all the premium features and study tools.

* * *

Who can join my Family plan?

Anyone can join your Quizlet Family plan, no matter where they live, as long as they have a Quizlet account.If you invite someone who already has an active subscription (Quizlet Plus or Quizlet Plus Unlimited), they'll need to turn off automatic renewal of their subscription and wait for their current plan to expire before they can join your family plan.

* * *

I'm currently paying for Quizlet Plus or Quizlet Plus Unlimited. Can I upgrade to a Quizlet Family plan or join one?

No, if you'd like to join a Quizlet Family plan, turn off auto-renewal on your current subscription and wait for it to expire. Once it does, you'll be able to upgrade to or join a Quizlet Family plan.
